The Open Development Toolkit is a project from Development Initiatives and Open Knowledge, which began in January 2014. The Toolkit will act as a hub around open development, bringing together tools and training materials in one place, with the aim of increasing use of open development data and encouraging its use to inform decisions within the sector.
